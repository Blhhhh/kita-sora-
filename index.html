✦ A Letter From Lynn ✦

Welcome to Kita-Sora

Dear traveler,

Kita-Sora means The North Sky.

It began as a small place I carried with me during a time when life felt uncertain and unfamiliar.

What started as fragments of memories, photographs, drawings, and quiet reflections slowly became a world of its own.

A world where seasons change gently.
Where ordinary moments are allowed to matter.
Where growing older does not mean leaving wonder behind.

This space is still becoming.

If something here feels familiar to you, perhaps you already belong here.

With love,

Lynn
